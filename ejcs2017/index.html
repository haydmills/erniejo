Resting Pitch Face

Resting Pitch Face (RPF) is a co-ed acapella group at Indiana University Bloomington that sings music of all types for people of all ages. The group aims to perform at as many events as it can on and off campus. It strives to have fun and provide entertainment to all. It also aims to improve its overall skill as an acapella group and as individual singers. While auditions are open to all IUB students, RPF is a place where non-music majors can express their passion for music!








XAcapella

XAcapella is a brand new a capella group consisting of students in Chi Alpha Christian Fellowship, an on-campus ministry. The EJCS will be their first official performance, and they're excited to be a part of the show!

Original Musical

This year's musical is "Fedor: A Grilling a Murderer Story". Taking place in the world of Ernie Jo's original series "Grilling a Murderer", it'll follow Detectives Fipps and Yager as they investigate some mysterious dissappearances at Christmas time, while Mayor Nally makes things difficult (as usual). The musical will be accompanied by a live orchestra directed by Jacobs School of Music PhD student Kenji Kuriyama. They'll be playing some Christmas favorites as well as a couple original songs.
