Ernest Joseph Fipps IV

Who is Ernie Jo?

Co-Founder of Mills Digital, YouTube Extraordinaire. Lover of cats, Halo, and Chick-fil-a. I moonlight as a vocal artist.

I grew up playing with a camera, but it wasn't until my senior year of high school that I fell in love with video editing. I didn't fully realize it at the time, but a couple years later I started a YouTube channel and decided I was going to be a videographer. And that's what I am dangit.

Whether I'm shooting a project for a client or making videos of a stuffed rhinoceros doing nothing, I'm constantly creating. And occasionally I even turn that creative energy towards music, or for extra fun I combine the two.

I'm always looking for people to make things with or to make things for, so hit me up yo.
