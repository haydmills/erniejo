Resting Pitch Face

Resting Pitch Face (RPF) is a co-ed acapella group at Indiana University Bloomington that sings music of all types for people of all ages. The group aims to perform at as many events as it can on and off campus. It strives to have fun and provide entertainment to all. It also aims to improve its overall skill as an acapella group and as individual singers. While auditions are open to all IUB students, RPF is a place where non-music majors can express their passion for music!









Movement Exchange

We are dancers without borders. We are activists and global citizens, movers and shakers, dreamers and achievers, teachers and learners, volunteers and friends. And we believe that sharing movement through music and expression is a powerful tool for changing the world into a better place. Movement Exchange at IU’s mission is to provide movement and arts programming for underserved populations, both locally and internationally. We are the Indiana University chapter of the national non-profit that started it all, Movement Exchange.

Boney Junes

We’re Boney Junes. We began 7 years ago on a walk from Josh’s basement to the Kangaroo gas station down the street. It is on this walk that we wrote our first song together. While we’ve kept rather quiet since, we haven’t stopped working. Whether it’s writing, performing, or investing in our own studio equipment, we have continued to give the dream that we have in our heads the best chance there is to come to life. Seven years have passed, and we believe that we have arrived at something that does just that. It’s called “Cemetery Cars.” We hope you enjoy it.








Hoosier Pops Orchestra

The Hoosier Pops Orchestra at Indiana University is an undergraduate student orchestra comprised of undergraduate students from the Jacobs School of Music and all majors from IU who are interested and committed to performing orchestral Pops repertoire to a high quality. With opportunities for artistic management, conducting, and performing, this organization is meant to enhance the undergraduate musical experience. We have one concert per semester in addition to weekly rehearsals and monthly social events.
