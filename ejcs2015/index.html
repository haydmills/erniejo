Jo

Jo (played by Ernie Jo) is an awesome, cat-loving, Christmas fanatic. He's fun loving and kind of crazy, and gets passionate about things. Jo used to date Clarice, until she dumped him last Christmas to date Ralphie, a man with the eyes of a newborn egret. He's now dating Jovie, who's a little less keen on Jo's wild antics, but she still supports him in everything he does.

×

Jovie

Jovie has grown up with Jo but never really saw him as anything more than a friend until Clarice kicked him to the curb last Christmas. She scooped him up and helped him through his messy breakup. She's more reserved than Jo and doesn't always understand his craziness. That being said, she is a loyal girlfriend who isn't afraid of commitment.

×

Clarice

Clarice is a gentle, caring girl who loves Christmas, but maybe not as much as Jo. She and Jo used to date and for the most part she embraced his strangeness. Once she met Ralphie though, his boy-ish good looks and silky-smooth skin drew her away from Jo and their hometown. She, like the rest of Jo's friends, has a knocking problem. Her grandpa owns the local Domino's, and is excited that Clarice will be in town for the holidays.

×

Betty

Betty is good friends with Clarice, and is her main gal-pal even though she may not always understand what goes on in Clarice's head. She's in a drumming club with Jo, and is pretty easy-going. She cares about her friends and tries to help them through their drama. Betty is pumped to see Clarice while she's in town for the holidays.

×

Charlie

Charlie is Jo's best bud. He's a laid-back guy who tries to provide comic relief while also maintaining a safe distance from drama. He doesn't always offer much practical help to Jo, but he at least supports him emotionally. He's a fan of superheroes and Chick-fil-a. Tact isn't his strong suit.

×

Cindy

Cindy is Jovie's number 2, but she's pretty good friends with just about everyone in town. She tries to be the voice of reason when troubles come around, but she's softspoken at times. She's overall just a good friend to have around, and will be there for you no matter what. Her grandma makes the best cookies around, and is planning on opening a store with the money she won in the lawsuit against Santa's Wonderland in the mall last Christmas.

×

Ralphie

Ralphie is the seemingly young buck who entranced Clarice with his dang boy-ish charm. He's got a youthful look that just melts girls like butter on a warm summer's day.

×

Russell

Russell is Ralphie's identical twin brother. He's got the same silky smooth skin and strong jaw-line that make Ralphie irresistable. He's morally compromised.
