In the spring of 2014, Ernie Jo, world renowned vocal artist, took his talents to Indiana University for a food court tour titled the For the People Tour. What once was just a dare has turned in to a cultural revolution. Ernie Jo has changed lives one cafeteria at a time. This is tour was truly For the People.
